Tirupathi

Tirupati, also known as Tirumala, is a famous pilgrimage destination located in the Chittoor district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It is renowned for the Sri Venkateswara Temple, which is one of the most visited and revered Hindu temples in world.
